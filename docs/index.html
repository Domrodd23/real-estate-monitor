Price forecast — public-data model

A transparent public-data forecast with a stated track record — not a prediction, not a guarantee, and not any paid service's score. Two methods are reported per ZIP, each next to its own backtest error: a per-ZIP time-series model (ETS on ZHVI history) and a national driver model, plus their blend.

National driver model: trained on 400 ZIPs, out-of-time test of 3200 ZIP-months — mean absolute error 3.1 pp on the 12-month change, directional accuracy 74%. Top drivers (permutation importance): mort_chg12 (0.428), mom3 (0.404), rel_level (0.04), mom12 (0.038), mom6 (0.019), ptr (0.006).
